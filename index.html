Welcome, I'm Felicia

I am a Central New Jersey-based UX/UI Designer, with a focus on turning problems into research-based solutions. I have a growth and empathic mindset, with a desire to create meaningful user experiences.

My Projects

Vaultage

Cloud Storage App

A clickable high fidelity prototype. It was usability tested on several potential users. This prototype addresses the initial pain points of cloud service users based on data research.

Bloc Jams

Desktop Music App

A fully functional music player. Displaying album information, list the songs for the album. Includes standard music controls for playing, pausing, skipping forward, backward, and adjusting the volume. I was tasked with adding various frontend features.

Busy Bus

Mobile App

BusyBus is a single screen prototype solution to the problem identified by Transit Officials at the Washington & State Street Bus stop. Riders wanted the ability to know when their exact bus would be arriving into the station.
